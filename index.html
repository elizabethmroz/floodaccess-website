Understand how flooding affects access

FloodAccess is a flood-adjusted accessibility modelling tool designed for researchers, planners and emergency managers. It evaluates how flooding impacts access to user-defined destinations using network and raster-based analysis.

View Downloads

About

FloodAccess is currently under development.

The project is inspired by research into flood-related accessibility and aims to provide a flexible platform for modelling access to healthcare, education, emergency shelters, food supplies, workplaces and other critical destinations.

Methodology

  • Flood depth and velocity analysis
  • Road and transport network accessibility
  • Raster-based travel-time modelling
  • Reachable and isolated population analysis
  • GeoTIFF, GeoPackage and CSV outputs
  • Scenario-based comparison modelling
